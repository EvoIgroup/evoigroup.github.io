The Evolutionary Intelligence (EvoI) Group was founded in January 2023, affiliated with Xidian University (XDU), China. Generally, our research motivations can be interpreted at four levels: EvoI for Network Sciences (NS), EvoI for Machine Learning (ML), EvoI for Optimization (OP), and EvoI for Application (AP).

  • Causal Inference
  • Community Detection
